About Me

Hello I’m a 26-year-old, Freelance Front end web developer. One year ago i found my passion in front end devepment and the problem solving aspect of it, since then my goal has been to learn new technologies and skills so that I can build bigger and better things in the future.

The part i appreciate most about front end development is that i can build something out of nothing and make it look good.


In my design and my code i like to work with a big picture and clear goal in mind to create efficient and clear solutions in advance.

I enjoy solving complex and big problems in splitting them into multiple simple ones.

When I’m not developing websites , I work out or relax and draw illustations.
